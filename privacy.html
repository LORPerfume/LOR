PRIVACY POLICY

LOR PERFUME • LUXURY FRAGRANCE HOUSE

At LOR Perfume, privacy is not just a legal requirement — it is part of the trust we build with every customer who chooses our fragrance. We believe that luxury begins with respect, and that includes how we handle your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our website, we may collect basic personal details such as your name, phone number, and delivery address. This information is required only to complete your order successfully.

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used strictly to process orders, arrange delivery, and ensure a smooth customer experience. In some cases, we may use your contact details to update you about your order status.

3. Data Protection

We apply reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system can guarantee 100% security, and by using our website you acknowledge this reality.

4. Customer Trust

LOR Perfume is built on trust, elegance, and long-term customer relationships. Every decision we make is guided by one principle: your privacy and confidence matter.

We treat your information with the same care we put into crafting our fragrances.

5. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements in our services or legal requirements. Any changes will always be posted on this page.
