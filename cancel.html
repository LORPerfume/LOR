CANCELLATION POLICY

LOR PERFUME • ORDER MANAGEMENT

At LOR Perfume, we prepare every order carefully to ensure a smooth delivery experience. For this reason, cancellation requests are handled according to the conditions below.

1. Cancellation Before Shipment

Orders can be cancelled only before shipment. Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible.

Please contact our customer support as soon as possible if you need to cancel your order.

2. After Dispatch

After the order leaves our facility and is on its way to the customer, we cannot accept cancellation requests.

3. Contact Support

For cancellation requests, customers should provide their order details and contact information to help us process the request quickly.
