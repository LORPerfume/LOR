REFUND & RETURNS POLICY

At LOR Perfume, customer satisfaction is one of our highest priorities. We carefully inspect and prepare every product before shipment.

Refunds are accepted only in cases of damaged products or receiving an incorrect item.

Return requests must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery. Products must be unused and in their original condition.

Your experience with LOR Perfume matters to us, and we always work to provide a fair solution.

3. Non-Eligible Returns

Products that have been used, opened, or damaged after delivery may not qualify for return or refund.

4. Support

For any issue related to your order, please contact our customer service team with your order details.
